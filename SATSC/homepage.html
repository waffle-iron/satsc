Who we are?

The Sta. Ana Transport Service Cooperative is a cooperative, officially accredited by the Land Transportation Franchising and Regulatory Board (LTFRB) and the Land Transportation Office, which fully handles jeepney operators and drivers that are franchised or routed around the vicinity of Sta. Ana, Manila. The cooperative assists newly-applying drivers in acquiring their own jeepneys and also aids them in their financial needs. They also help jeepney operators search for jeepney drivers that fit their standards and qualifications.
